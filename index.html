    ABOUT US

    We educate a number of children

    Life Goals Foundation supports children from ages 7 to 18 years, currently we are supporting 24 children get through school. We have children enrolled in classes Primary 1 to Primary 7, as well as secondary with afew Senior 4 candidates. Life Goals is focused on educating Africa both financially and academically. Life Goals pays for these children's tuition, sanitary pads, school requirements among other things, to ensure their peace of mind while at school.
